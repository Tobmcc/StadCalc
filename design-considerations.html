Wheelchair & companion provision SGSA Accessible Stadia 2003
WCU positions: under 10,000 = min(6, 1 per 100); 10,000–20,000 = 100 + 5/1,000 above 10k; 20,000–40,000 = 150 + 3/1,000 above 20k; 40,000+ = 210 + 2/1,000 above 40k. WCU WCs = positions ÷ 15, rounded up at every band.
A companion seat is provided directly beside each wheelchair space, so companion count tracks WCU Positions 1:1.
Amenity seating SGSA Accessible Stadia 2015 supplementary guidance
Amenity and easy access spaces are not wheelchair spaces — they're standard seats reserved for ambulant disabled spectators who can transfer from a wheelchair or walk short distances but need easier, step-free access (typically near entrances/exits, with a wider seatway and fewer steps to reach). Calculated independently from wheelchair provision, using the same sliding-scale structure against total capacity.
